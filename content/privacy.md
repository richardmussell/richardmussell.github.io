## 2. Data Collection and Processing

### 2.1 Static Website Nature

This is a **static website** built with Hugo and hosted on GitHub Pages. This means:

- ✅ **No databases** - We do not store personal data in any database
- ✅ **No server-side processing** - All content is pre-generated static files
- ✅ **No user accounts** - No registration or login functionality
- ✅ **No cookies** - We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

#### GitHub Pages Logging

When you visit this website, **GitHub Pages automatically logs certain information** for operational and security purposes:

- **IP Address** - Your Internet Protocol address
- **Browser Type** - The web browser you are using
- **Access Timestamp** - Date and time of your visit
- **Pages Visited** - Which pages you accessed
- **Referrer URL** - The website that referred you (if applicable)

**Purpose:** This information is collected by GitHub Pages (not by us) for:
- Security and fraud prevention
- Website performance monitoring
- Access analytics

**Data Controller:** GitHub, Inc. (see [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement))

**Retention:** GitHub retains this data according to their privacy policy. We do not have direct access to these logs.

### 2.3 Contact Form Data

If you use the contact form on this website:

**Service Used:** Formspree (formspree.io)

**Data Collected:**
- Name (as provided)
- Email address (as provided)
- Message content (as provided)

**Purpose:** To respond to your inquiries and communicate with you.

**Data Processing:**
- Form submissions are processed by Formspree
- Formspree forwards submissions to the website owner's email
- Data is stored temporarily by Formspree for delivery purposes

**Data Controller:** Formspree (see [Formspree Privacy Policy](https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy))

**Retention:** Formspree retains form submissions according to their privacy policy. We recommend reviewing their privacy policy for specific retention periods.

**Your Rights:** You can request deletion of your form submission data by contacting us (see Section 7).

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Under GDPR (if you are in the EU) and CCPA (if you are in California), you have the following rights:

### 5.1 Right to Access
You have the right to request information about what personal data we hold about you.

### 5.2 Right to Rectification
You have the right to request correction of inaccurate personal data.

### 5.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
You have the right to request deletion of your personal data.

### 5.4 Right to Restrict Processing
You have the right to request restriction of processing of your personal data.

### 5.5 Right to Data Portability
You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used format.

### 5.6 Right to Object
You have the right to object to processing of your personal data.

### 5.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
If processing is based on consent, you have the right to withdraw consent at any time.

**How to Exercise Your Rights:** Contact us using the information in Section 7. We will respond to your request within 30 days (or as required by applicable law).
